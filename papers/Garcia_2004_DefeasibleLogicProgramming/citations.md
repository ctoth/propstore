## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Stolzenburg et al. (2003)** "Computing generalized specificity" -- Formal details of the specificity comparison criterion used in DeLP. Essential for implementing the argument comparison module. *(p.13)*
- **Chesnevar et al. (2003)** "An abstract machine for defeasible argumentation" -- The ODAM implementation details. Relevant for efficient dialectical tree construction. *(p.34)*
- **Prakken and Sartor (1997)** "Argument-based logic programming with defeasible priorities" -- The main comparison system, uses dialectical proof theory with different architecture. Important for understanding design space. *(p.35)*
- **Brewka (2001)** "On the relation between defeasible logic and well-founded semantics" -- Connects DeLP-style reasoning to LP semantics. Relevant for understanding formal properties. *(p.37)*
- **Garcia (2000)** PhD thesis on DeLP -- Full formal development including proofs, parallel implementation, and complexity analysis not covered in this paper. *(p.34)*
