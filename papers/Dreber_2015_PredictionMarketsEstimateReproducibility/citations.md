## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Open Science Collaboration (2015)** — The RPP project providing the 44 studies used in these prediction markets. Already in collection as Aarts_2015.
- **Camerer et al. (2016)** — Follow-up applying prediction markets to economics replications with 18 studies. Already in collection.
- **Arrow et al. (2008)** — Foundational argument for prediction markets in science; relevant to understanding market mechanism design.
- **Hanson (2007)** — LMSR (logarithmic market scoring rule) used as the automated market maker in this study; relevant for understanding the pricing mechanism.
- **Ioannidis (2005)** — Foundational paper on false positive rates in science; provides the base rate framework that Dreber et al.'s Bayesian analysis builds on.
