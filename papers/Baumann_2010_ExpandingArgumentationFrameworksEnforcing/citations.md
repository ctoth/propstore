## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Cayrol, de Saint-Cyr, Lagasquie-Schiex 2008 [5]** — Revision of an argumentation system. Most directly related prior work on AF dynamics and revision operations. Worth reading for comparison of revision vs expansion approaches.

2. **Boella, Kaci, van der Torre 2009 [2]** — Dynamics in argumentation with single extensions. Studies what happens when arguments/attacks are added or removed, complementary perspective to this paper.

3. **Oikarinen and Woltran 2008 [14]** — Strong equivalence for AFs. The normal expansion concept used in this paper originated from their work on equivalence checking.

4. **Baroni and Giacomin 2007 [7]** — Principle-based evaluation (already in project corpus). The abstract principles (admissibility, reinstatement, directionality) used throughout this paper come from this work.

5. **Rotstein et al. 2008 [18]** — Argument theory change: revision upon warrant. An AGM-style approach to argumentation revision that complements the expansion approach taken here.
