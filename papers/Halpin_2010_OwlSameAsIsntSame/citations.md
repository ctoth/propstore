## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Ding et al. (2010) [ref 4]** - Parallel empirical study of owl:sameAs in Linked Data; complementary findings on misuse patterns and scale.
- **Bouquet et al. (2008) [ref 2]** - Entity Name System proposal; alternative architecture for identity resolution on the web of data.
- **McCusker & McGuinness (2010) [ref 12]** - Companion work by co-authors on identity in linked data; likely has deeper treatment of formal identity criteria.
- **Nikolov et al. (2007) [ref 15]** - Knofuss knowledge fusion architecture; relevant to how propstore handles merging claims from multiple sources.
- **Volz et al. (2009) [ref 17]** - Link discovery and maintenance; practical approaches to finding and maintaining identity links at scale.
