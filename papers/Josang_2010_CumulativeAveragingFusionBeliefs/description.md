Defines the multiplicative product of multinomial subjective opinions, extending Josang's subjective logic framework from binary to K-ary opinions over Cartesian product frames. Two methods are proposed for redistributing belief mass from overlapping subsets to singletons: "Assumed Belief Mass" (aggressive, less uncertainty) and "Assumed Uncertainty" (conservative, recommended). Provides the formal multinomial opinion representation needed for propstore's multi-valued stance classification, though fusion operators for combining same-frame opinions are covered in a separate Josang publication.
