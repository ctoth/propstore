6. M. Smithson. *Ignorance and Uncertainty: Emerging Paradigms*. Springer, 1984.

## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Josang 2001 — "A Logic for Uncertain Probabilities"**: The foundational binary opinion paper. Already implemented in propstore. Provides the Beta distribution mapping that this paper's multinomial work generalizes to Dirichlet.
- **[2] Josang 2008 — "Conditional Reasoning with Subjective Logic"**: Conditional operations on opinions — needed for dependent reasoning chains in propstore.
- **[3] Josang & McAnally 2004 — "Multiplication and Comultiplication of Beliefs"**: Earlier version of the multiplication operator for general (non-multinomial) beliefs. Shows the evolution of this approach.
- **[4] Josang, O'Hara & O'Grady 2010 — "Base Rates for Belief Functions"**: Base rate theory — directly relevant to propstore's prior stance distributions.
