## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Ballester-Ripoll & Leonelli 2022a** — Computing Sobol indices in probabilistic graphical models. The foundational exact algorithm this paper builds upon.
- **Saltelli et al. 2019** — Why so many published sensitivity analyses are false. Essential critique of OAT practices that motivates global SA.
- **Oseledets 2011** — Tensor-train decomposition. Mathematical foundation for the TT format used throughout.
- **Sobol 1990** — Original Sobol indices paper. Foundational for the variance decomposition framework.
- **Gray & Chan 2024** — Approximate tensor contraction. Future direction for scaling to larger networks.
