## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Halpern and Pearl (2005)** "Causes and Explanations" -- Updated definition of actual causation that addresses problems with the original Pearl 2000 definition; directly relevant to propstore's causal claim evaluation
- **Tian and Pearl (2002)** "On the Identification of Causal Effects" -- Completeness proof for do-calculus in semi-Markovian models; essential for understanding the limits of causal identification
- **Spirtes, Glymour, and Scheines (1993)** "Causation, Prediction, and Search" -- Alternative causal discovery framework; relevant for automated structure learning from data
- **Bareinboim and Pearl (2016)** "Causal Inference and the Data-Fusion Problem" -- Transportability of causal effects across domains; relevant for propstore's multi-source reasoning
- **Galles and Pearl (1998)** "An Axiomatic Characterization of Causal Counterfactuals" -- Formal axiom system for counterfactuals underlying Chapter 7; connects to belief revision (AGM)
