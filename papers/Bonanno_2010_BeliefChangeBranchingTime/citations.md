## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[8] Bonanno 2007** — The foundational paper introducing branching-time belief revision frames and their modal logic. Essential prerequisite for understanding the present paper's framework.
- **[13] Darwiche & Pearl 1997** — Iterated belief revision postulates (DP1-DP4). The standard reference for iterated revision principles that Bonanno's ternary functions can express.
- **[29] Nayak, Pagnucco & Peppas 2003** — Dynamic belief revision operators with detailed analysis of REFstrong and related iterated revision principles. Provides the "Conjunction" postulate.
- **[21] Hansson 1968** — Choice structures and preference relations. The choice-theoretic foundation underlying the rationalizability results (Proposition 15 in the appendix).
- **[1] Alchourrón, Gärdenfors & Makinson 1985** — The original AGM paper. Already in propstore's scope via Gärdenfors 1988.
