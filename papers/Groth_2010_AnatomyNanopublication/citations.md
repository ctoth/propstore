## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Carroll et al. 2005** --- Named graphs, provenance and trust. Foundational for the Named Graph mechanism that nanopublications are built on. Already a lead from Clark_2014.
- **[2] Ciccarese et al. 2008** --- SWAN biomedical discourse ontology. The annotation vocabulary substrate for nanopublications. Already a lead from Clark_2014.
- **[4] Mons & Velterop 2009** --- Earlier formulation of the nanopublication concept at SWASD workshop. Provides historical context for how the idea evolved.
- **[3] Groza et al. 2009** --- Survey of discourse representation models. Useful for comparing nanopublication model against alternatives.
