## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Clark et al. 2014 - Micropublications**: Already in collection. Defines semantic model for representing scientific claims with evidence/provenance -- directly related to propstore's claim architecture.
- **Starr et al. 2015 - Achieving human and machine accessibility of cited data**: Provides practical approaches to data citation that complement FAIR's F1/F3 principles on persistent identifiers and metadata-data linkage.
- **Bechhofer et al. 2013 - Why linked data is not enough for scientists**: Critiques linked-data-only approaches, arguing additional infrastructure (provenance, versioning, bundling) is needed -- relevant to propstore's beyond-RDF design choices.
- **Buneman et al. 2001 - Why and where: characterization of data provenance**: Foundational provenance theory that underpins FAIR's R1.2 principle -- directly relevant to propstore's provenance tracking.
- **Borgman 2015 - Big Data, Little Data, No Data**: Broader scholarly context on data stewardship challenges -- useful background for understanding the ecosystem FAIR addresses.
