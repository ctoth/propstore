Final JAIR version of the KR 2023 work on reasoning under incomplete information in ASPIC+. The paper formalizes four justification statuses over incomplete ASPIC+ theories, proves that stability is coNP-complete and relevance is Sigma_2^P-complete under grounded semantics, and gives ASP-based exact algorithms that scale on both a real-world police inquiry benchmark and synthetic data. Compared with the preliminary version, it adds general complexity bounds, a full relevance-all-queryables algorithm, a revised implementation, and a careful comparison to incomplete abstract argumentation frameworks.
