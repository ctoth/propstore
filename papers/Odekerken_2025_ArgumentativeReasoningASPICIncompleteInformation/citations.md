## Notes

The paper’s most important lead-generating references are the ASPIC+ tutorial, the incomplete argumentation-framework papers, the CEGAR papers, and the earlier KR 2023 version of this work. The remainder of the bibliography is mostly background on argumentation, ASP, and inquiry-style explanation systems.
