Adapts the DF-QuAD algorithm from QuAD frameworks to Bipolar Argumentation Frameworks (BAFs) by defining strength aggregation, mediating, and score functions that eliminate the need for base scores, using a fixed default of 0.5. Proves continuity of the semantics and equivalence with QuAD when base score is 0.5, and compares with social model gradual valuation. Directly relevant to propstore's DF-QuAD implementation, providing the formal basis for BAF-native gradual evaluation and testable invariants (saturation, extreme values, closed-form expression).
