## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Banarescu et al. 2013** — The original AMR specification. Essential for understanding what AMR represents and its design choices for English.
- **Cai and Knight 2013** — Defines the Smatch metric used throughout this paper. The foundational evaluation tool for all AMR work.
- **Damonte and Cohen 2018, 2020** — Cross-lingual AMR parsing evaluation and AMR 2.0 Four Translations dataset. Direct predecessors to this work's cross-lingual investigation.
- **Brisker and Carpuat 2020** — The main comparison system for divergence detection. Their string-level approach provides the baseline that AMR-based detection outperforms.
- **Xue et al. 2014** — First systematic comparison of English AMRs to Chinese and Czech, establishing that cross-lingual AMR is a viable research direction but noting significant structural differences.
