## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[15] Forbus 1984 - Qualitative process theory**: Companion paper in the same AI volume; presents an alternative approach to qualitative physics based on processes rather than components. Essential comparison for understanding the landscape of qualitative reasoning.
- **[21] Kuipers 1984 - Commonsense reasoning about causality**: Another companion paper in the same volume; provides a complementary approach to deriving behavior from structure.
- **[8] De Kleer & Sussman 1980 - Propagation of constraints**: Earlier work on constraint propagation in circuit synthesis that directly influenced the confluence framework.
- **[7] De Kleer & Brown 1983 - Assumptions and ambiguities in mechanistic mental models**: Develops the ideas of assumptions and multiple interpretations in qualitative reasoning, directly foreshadowing the ATMS.
- **[20] Karnopp & Rosenberg 1975 - System Dynamics**: The system dynamics formalism (bond graphs) that provides the quantitative foundation from which the qualitative models are derived.
