## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Beek, Schlobach, and van Harmelen (2016) - Direct formal precursor for contextualized `owl:sameAs`; likely important for propstore identity policies.
- Batchelor et al. (2014) and Brenninkmeijer et al. (2012) - Scientific lenses in OpenPHACTS, the closest prior implementation path this paper extends.
- Nguyen, Bodenreider, and Sheth (2014) - Singleton properties, the representation used here for statement-level correspondence metadata.
- Halpin et al. (2010) - Foundational analysis of why `owl:sameAs` is often too strong for linked data identity.
- Volz et al. (2009), SILK - Link discovery tooling whose outputs can be enriched and reused in the Lenticular Lens framework.
