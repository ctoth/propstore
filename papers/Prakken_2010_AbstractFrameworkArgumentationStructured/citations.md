## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Caminada and Amgoud (2007)** "On the evaluation of argumentation formalisms" -- the key paper motivating rationality postulates; this work extends their results to the present framework
- **Modgil and Prakken (2014)** "The ASPIC+ framework for structured argumentation" -- the successor to this paper, extending it with preference handling and broader applicability
- **Bondarenko et al. (1997)** "An abstract, argumentation-theoretic approach to default reasoning" -- defines ABA, shown here to be a special case of ASPIC
- **Gordon et al. (2007)** "The Carneades model of argument and burden of proof" -- related structured argumentation framework with distinct approach to proof standards
- **Vreeswijk (1997)** "Abstract argumentation systems" -- key predecessor framework that this work builds upon and generalizes
