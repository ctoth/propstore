HERMES is a web-based collaborative decision support system that integrates argumentation discourse (issues, alternatives, positions, constraints) with multi-criteria weighting and scoring mechanisms. The system extends IBIS-style argumentation with quantitative proof standards (scintilla of evidence, preponderance, beyond reasonable doubt, dialectical validity) and automated constraint consistency checking, providing a hybrid framework where argumentative deliberation and formal decision analysis operate over the same structured discussion graph.
