## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Pustejovsky and Moszkowicz 2011** -- "The qualitative spatial dynamics of motion in language" -- Core predecessor defining the frame-based event structures that DES builds on. Essential for understanding the formal foundations.
- **Harel, Tiuryn, and Kozen 2000** -- "Dynamic Logic" -- The formal logic framework (PDL) underlying the temporal reasoning in DES. Important for any computational implementation.
- **Pustejovsky and Jezek, forthcoming** -- "Verbal Patterns of Change" -- The promised full treatment of DES with complete formal development. Would contain the missing axiomatization.
- **Pustejovsky 2012** -- "The Semantics of Functional Spaces" -- Related work on spatial semantics that connects to habitat theory's treatment of contextual preconditions.
- **Kennedy 1999 / Hay, Kennedy, Levin 1999** -- Gradability and scale structure in degree achievements -- Foundation for the scale-typed attribute system used in DES.
