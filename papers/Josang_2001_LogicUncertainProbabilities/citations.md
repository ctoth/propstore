## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Shafer 1976 (#4)** — Foundational Dempster-Shafer theory that subjective logic builds upon. Essential for understanding belief mass assignments and combination rules.
- **Josang 1997 (#3)** — Earlier version of subjective logic applied to authentication. Shows practical application to trust networks.
- **Josang 1999 (#11)** — Trust-based decision making for electronic transactions. Directly relevant to trust chain modeling in propstore.
- **Walley 1997 (#16)** — Alternative uncertain probability framework using second-order possibility distributions. Worth comparing approaches.
- **Smets 1993 (#6)** — Transferable belief model with open-world assumption. Alternative combination rule philosophy.
