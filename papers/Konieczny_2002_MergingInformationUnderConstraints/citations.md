## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Alchourron, Gardenfors, Makinson 1985** — AGM postulates for belief revision; IC merging postulates are designed as a generalization. Already in propstore as `Alchourron_1985_TheoryChange`.
- **[18] Katsuno & Mendelzon 1992** — KM revision postulates shown to be special case of IC merging (singleton belief set). The bridge between revision and merging is via this paper.
- **[22] Konieczny & Pino Perez 1998** — Precursor paper on pure merging (no integrity constraints); the IC framework extends this. Contains the original postulates A1-A6 that IC0-IC8 generalize.
- **[24/25] Liberatore & Schaerf 1995/1998** — Commutative revision operators shown to be a special case of IC merging on 2-element belief sets. Important for understanding the relationship between pairwise and n-ary merging.
- **[8] Dalal 1988** — Distance-based revision using Hamming distance between interpretations. Used as the concrete distance function in all examples in this paper.
