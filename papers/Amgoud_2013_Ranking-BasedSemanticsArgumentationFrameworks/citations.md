## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[4] Cayrol & Lagasquie-Schiex 2005** --- "Graduality in Argumentation" --- the main prior work on gradual acceptability; extends extension-based semantics with weighted attacks. Different approach from ranking-based semantics (augments existing rather than replacing).
- **[2] Baroni & Giacomin 2007** --- "On principle-based evaluation of extension-based argumentation semantics" --- the methodological inspiration for this paper; applies axiomatic evaluation to extension-based semantics. Already in collection.
- **[7] Dunne et al. 2011** --- "Weighted argument systems" --- weighted argumentation where weights are externally provided rather than derived from structure. Contrast to the structural approach here.
- **[6] Dung 1995** --- The foundational paper on abstract argumentation frameworks. Already in collection.
- **[1] Amgoud & Vesic 2011** --- "A new approach for preference-based argumentation frameworks" --- same first author's prior work on preference-based argumentation. Already in collection.
