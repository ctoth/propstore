## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Smets (1992b) IRIDIA TR** — full proofs of every theorem in this paper. Essential if propstore wants to encode these axioms formally.
- **Smets and Kennes (1990) "The Transferable Belief Model"** — already in propstore as `papers/Smets_Kennes_1994_TransferableBeliefModel/`. The TBM paper this 1993 work axiomatizes.
- **Smets (1990b) Pignistic transformation** — the credal-to-pignistic projection used at decision time. Maps onto propstore's render-time policy decisions.
- **Gardenfors (1988) "Knowledge in Flux"** — homomorphism and preservation conditions are imported from this work; relevant for AGM revision in `propstore.belief_set`.
- **Klawonn and Schwecke (1992)** — companion axiomatic justification specifically for Dempster's rule of combination (vs. this paper which axiomatizes the credibility function itself).
- **Shafer (1976)** — already in propstore as `papers/Shafer_1976_MathematicalTheoryEvidence/`. Original belief-function reference.
