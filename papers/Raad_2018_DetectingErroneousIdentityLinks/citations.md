## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Beek et al. 2018, sameAs.cc: supplies the closure/equality-set infrastructure that this paper uses for partitioning and publication of identity sets.
- Guéret et al. 2012: direct prior network-measures approach for linked-data mapping quality.
- Halpin et al. 2010: core critique of `owl:sameAs` misuse and identity semantics in linked data.
- Valdestilhas et al. 2017: scalable erroneous-link detection in large link repositories, useful as a comparison point for runtime and detection assumptions.
- Raad et al. 2017: related contextual-identity work by the same author group, useful for connecting topology-only scoring to context-specific identity.
