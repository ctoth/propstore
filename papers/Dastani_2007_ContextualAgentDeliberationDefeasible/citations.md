## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[3] Dastani, Governatori, Rotolo, van der Torre (2005), Programming cognitive agents in defeasible logic, LPAR 2005, LNAI 3835:621-636.** — Direct predecessor introducing the modal rule types this paper extends with meta-rules and ⊗-on-rules. Read first for context.
- **[9] Song and Governatori (2005), Nested rules in defeasible logic, RuleML 2005, LNCS 3791:204-208.** — Technical antecedent of the meta-rule device; the formal nesting machinery here generalises that paper.
- **[6] Governatori and Rotolo (2006), Logic of violations, Australasian J. Logic 4:193-215.** — Source of the ⊗-on-literals connective (PREF in Def. 1) that this paper lifts to rules.
- **[5] Governatori and Rotolo (2004), Defeasible logic: Agency, intention and obligation, Δeon'04, LNCS 3065:114-128.** — Foundations of the agency / intention / obligation modal layer.
- **[4] Governatori, Padmanabhan, Rotolo (2006), Rule-based agents in temporalised defeasible logic, PRICAI 2006, LNAI 4099:31-40.** — The temporal counterpart cited as the BDI-CTL alternative whose complexity the present paper aims to avoid.
