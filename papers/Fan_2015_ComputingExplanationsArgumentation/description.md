Introduces "related admissibility," a new argumentation semantics that filters admissible sets to exclude arguments irrelevant to explaining why a given argument is acceptable, applicable to both Abstract Argumentation and Assumption-based Argumentation.
The paper defines minimal, compact, and maximal explanation types and provides a sound and complete computational mechanism using dispute forests.
Directly relevant to propstore's argumentation and render layers for generating explanations of claim acceptance/rejection in argumentation frameworks.
