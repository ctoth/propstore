## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dung, Kowalski, and Toni 2006** — Foundational work on dialectic proof procedures for assumption-based admissible argumentation. The dispute tree mechanism that Fan & Toni 2015 extends into dispute forests originates here.

- **Garcia, Chesnevar, Rotstein, and Simari 2013** — Formalizes dialectical explanation support for argument-based reasoning in knowledge-based systems. Uses "explanations" differently (sets of trees) but addresses the same general problem of explaining argumentation outcomes.

- **Schulz and Toni 2013** — Uses ABA to explain answer set membership. Related approach but doesn't formalize explanation as a semantic property.

- **Toni 2014** — Tutorial on ABA providing the background framework that Fan & Toni 2015 builds upon. Essential for understanding the ABA formalism used in the paper.

- **Baroni and Giacomin 2007** — Principle-based evaluation of argumentation semantics. Provides the evaluation criteria framework that could be applied to assess related admissibility.
