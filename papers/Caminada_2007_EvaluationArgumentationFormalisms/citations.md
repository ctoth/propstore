## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[42] Dung 1995** - The foundational abstract argumentation framework that all work in this paper builds upon. Already in the collection.
- **[4] Amgoud et al. 2004 (ASPIC Deliverable D2.2)** - The original ASPIC framework that this paper evaluates and fixes. The precursor to ASPIC+.
- **[39] Prakken & Sartor 1997** - The argumentation system shown to have rationality postulate violations. Important predecessor to ASPIC+.
- **[30] Garcia & Simari 2004** - DeLP (Defeasible Logic Programming), another formalism evaluated against the postulates, which partially but not fully satisfies them.
- **[27] Clark 1978 (Negation as failure)** - Connection between transposition closure and Clark completion for logic programs, mentioned as a related approach in the discussion.
