## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Doyle [12]** (1979) "A truth maintenance system" -- The original justification-based TMS that the ATMS improves upon. Essential for understanding what the ATMS replaces and why.
- **de Kleer [7]** (1986) "Extending the ATMS" -- Companion paper in same issue extending the basic ATMS to handle arbitrary propositional expressions (disjunction axioms), default reasoning styles.
- **de Kleer [8]** (1986) "Problem solving with the ATMS" -- Companion paper defining the problem-solver-TMS interface protocol, consumer architecture, and constraint language. Already in collection.
- **Martins [13]** (1983) "Reasoning in multiple belief spaces" -- Alternative approach to multi-context reasoning; de Kleer compares the ATMS to this system.
- **Stallman and Sussman [20]** (1977) "Forward reasoning and dependency-directed backtracking" -- Early work on dependency-directed backtracking that the ATMS builds upon and supersedes.
