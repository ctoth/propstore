## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Baumann & Brewka 2019 (JELIA)** — "AGM Meets Abstract Argumentation: Contraction for Dung Frameworks" — the companion paper covering AGM contraction for AFs, completing the AGM trilogy
- **Oikarinen & Woltran 2011** — Characterization of strong equivalence, foundational for the kernel operation that underlies all Dung logics
- **Cayrol, de Saint-Cyr, Lagasquie-Schiex 2009/2010** — Alternative AF dynamics approaches (adding arguments), contrasted with this paper's AGM approach
- **Diller et al. 2015** — Extension-based AF revision (concurrent IJCAI paper), an alternative to the model-based approach taken here
- **Coste-Marquis et al. 2014a** — Revision of argumentation systems via enforcement, the main competing approach discussed in Section 5.4
