## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[19] Dung (1995):** The semantic base layer. Every core definition in Sections 2-4 is formulated as an extension or repair of Dung's acceptability machinery.
- **[11] Bench-Capon (2003):** The main comparison point for value-based argumentation with fixed value orderings; Modgil's hierarchical EAF construction generalizes it by allowing meta-level arguments about the orderings themselves.
- **[24] Kakas and Moraitis (2003):** Important because Section 5 explicitly reconstructs value-based decision making inside hierarchical EAFs and contrasts externally supplied priorities with arguable ones.
- **[34] Prakken and Sartor (1997):** Essential for Section 7; the ALP-DP instantiation is one of the paper's main concrete demonstrations and a direct bridge into structured defeasible logic.
- **[28] Modgil (2007):** The immediate precursor, introducing an abstract theory of defeasible preference reasoning that this paper extends into the full EAF machinery and Dung-style semantics.
