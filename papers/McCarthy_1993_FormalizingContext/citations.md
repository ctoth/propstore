## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[Guha, 1991]** — R.V. Guha's PhD thesis is the most comprehensive development of McCarthy's context ideas, directly implementing them in Cyc. Essential for understanding the practical formalization.
- **[Buvac and Mason, 1993]** — Formal propositional logic of context, appearing at the same conference. Provides the rigorous logical foundations that McCarthy's notes sketch informally.
- **[Shoham, 1991]** — Analyzes context as a modal operator, providing an alternative formal perspective on ist(c, p) that may connect to modal logic-based argumentation frameworks.
- **[McCarthy, 1987]** — The original "Generality in AI" paper where McCarthy first proposes contexts. Required reading for understanding the motivation and trajectory.
- **[McCarthy and Hayes, 1969]** — Foundational paper on AI and philosophy that sets up the epistemological problems contexts are designed to solve.
