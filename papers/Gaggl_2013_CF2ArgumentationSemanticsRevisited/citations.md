## Key Follow-up

- **Baroni, Giacomin, and Guida (2005)**: the SCC-recursive schema that this paper revisits and simplifies.
- **Oikarinen and Woltran (2011)**: the strong-equivalence/kernels paper this article extends for cf2, stage, and naive semantics.
- **Dvorak and Woltran (2010)** and **Dunne and Caminada (2008)**: complexity baselines for stage/semi-stable semantics.
- **Egly, Gaggl, and Woltran (2010)**: the ASP encoding line that this paper’s fixed-point characterization feeds into.
