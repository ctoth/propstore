## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Baroni et al. 2015** — The original QuAD algorithm paper. Essential companion to understand what DF-QuAD improves upon. Contains the BBS graph formalism and the original strength aggregation function.
- **Cayrol and Lagasquie-Schiex 2005** — Foundational bipolar argumentation paper. Already in collection.
- **Leite and Martins 2011** — Social abstract argumentation where base scores come from vote aggregation. Relevant to understanding alternative base score sources.
- **Rago, Toni, and Cyras 2016 (SAFA/COMMA workshop)** — Extends DF-QuAD to general BAFs without the QuAD restriction. The companion paper to this one.
- **Gordon, Prakken, and Walton 2007** — Carneades model with proof burdens. Related argumentation framework for decision support.
