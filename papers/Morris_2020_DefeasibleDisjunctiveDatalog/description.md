Extends the KLM framework for defeasible reasoning from propositional logic to Disjunctive Datalog. Provides algorithmic definitions for Rational Closure, Lexicographic Closure, and Relevant Closure adapted to handle disjunction, variables, and constants in Datalog. Proves Rational and Lexicographic Closure are LM-rational (satisfy all expressible KLM properties) while Relevant Closure fails Or, CM, and RM. Introduces Datalog+ syntax with compound terms for full KLM property expressibility.
