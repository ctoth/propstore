## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Modgil and Prakken 2013** - Defines the ASPIC+ framework whose native minimality notion this paper scrutinizes.
- **Prakken 2010** - Background on structured arguments and abstracting them into triples and frameworks.
- **Besnard and Hunter 2001** - Canonical logic-based argumentation source for minimal-support style arguments, used here as a comparison point.
- **Garcia and Simari 2004** - DeLP is the comparison case where premises and rules are explicitly support-minimal.
- **Pollock 1987 / 1995 / 1996** - Pollock's line of work motivates why circular or redundant derivations are undesirable in practical argument systems.
