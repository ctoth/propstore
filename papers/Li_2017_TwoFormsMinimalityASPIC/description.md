Clarifies that ASPIC+ only guarantees a weak "everything included is used" notion of minimality, not the stronger closure-based minimality often assumed in structured argumentation. It formalizes circularity and redundancy, defines a stronger minimal or regular argument notion, and proves that these stronger arguments can be uniquely characterized by their grounds, rules, and conclusion. Directly relevant for propstore because it tells you when an argument object is canonical versus merely derivationally valid.
