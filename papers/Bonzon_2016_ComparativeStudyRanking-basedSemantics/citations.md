## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Amgoud and Ben-Naim 2013** — Defines the burden-based semantics and several axiomatic properties used in this comparison. Original source for many of the properties evaluated here.
- **Cayrol and Lagasquie-Schiex 2005** — Defines both the discussion-based semantics and the Tuples semantics. Foundational work on gradual argumentation.
- **Besnard and Hunter 2001** — Introduces the categoriser function. The simplest and most widely-used ranking semantics.
- **Leite and Martins 2011** — Introduces Social Abstract Argumentation Frameworks used in this paper to formalize several properties.
- **Matt and Toni 2008** — Game-theoretic approach to argument strength; one of the five semantics compared.
