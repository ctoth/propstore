## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Pearl [2000]** *Causality: Models, Reasoning, and Inference* — The comprehensive textbook treatment of structural causal models; essential background for implementing any causal reasoning system.
- **Halpern & Pearl [2001] Part II: Explanations** — Companion paper defining causal explanation on top of this causation definition; directly relevant to rendering explanations in propstore.
- **Hall [2002] "Two concepts of causation"** — Distinguishes "dependence" from "production" concepts of causation; important philosophical context for understanding what HP causality captures.
- **Lewis [2000] "Causation as influence"** — Lewis's response to preemption problems; useful contrast to the structural-model approach.
- **Eiter & Lukasiewicz [2001]** — Tractable cases for HP causality; essential for any implementation that needs to scale.
