## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Kuhn & Dumontier 2014 [18]** "Trusty URIs: Verifiable, Immutable, and Permanent Digital Artifacts for Linked Data" -- The foundational paper on trusty URIs (content-hash identifiers for Linked Data). Core technology this paper builds on.
- **Kuhn et al. 2016 [17]** "Decentralized provenance-aware publishing with nanopublications" -- Describes the decentralized nanopublication server network used for retrieval evaluation.
- **Wilkinson et al. 2016 [32]** "The FAIR guiding principles" -- The FAIR data principles that motivate verifiable, persistent dataset references.
- **Carroll et al. (named graphs/provenance)** -- Named graphs as an alternative approach to provenance in RDF.
- **Queralt-Rosinach et al. 2016 [31]** "Publishing DisGeNET as nanopublications" -- Application of nanopublication approach to the DisGeNET disease-gene dataset evaluated in this paper.
