## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dejl et al. 2021** — "Argflow: A toolkit for deep argumentative explanations for neural networks." Most advanced work on connecting deep learning with argumentation-based explanations via GA frameworks. Relevant for extending propstore's argumentation layer to explain ML-derived outputs.

- **Timmer et al. 2017** — "A two-phase method for extracting explanatory arguments from bayesian networks." Shows how to map probabilistic models to support argumentation frameworks, relevant for probabilistic argumentation integration in propstore.

- **Fan and Toni 2015b** — "On computing explanations in argumentation." Foundational work defining explanations as related admissible extensions in AA frameworks. Core formal machinery for any AF-based explanation system.

- **Vassiliades et al. 2021** — "Argumentation and Explainable Artificial Intelligence: A Survey." Complementary survey with broader scope (not restricted to AF-based approaches), published in Knowledge Engineering Review. Useful cross-reference.

- **Miller 2019** — "Explanation in artificial intelligence: Insights from the social sciences." Influential work grounding XAI in social science theories of explanation, cited extensively by this survey as motivation for why argumentation matches human explanation patterns.
