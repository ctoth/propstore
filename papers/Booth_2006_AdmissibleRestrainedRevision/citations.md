## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Darwiche & Pearl (1997)** - Foundational framework for iterated belief revision that this paper extends. Already referenced in propstore collection. Essential for understanding the baseline postulates.
- **Papini (2001)** - Backwards revision operator. Restrained revision is proven to be backwards revision + natural revision. Understanding this operator is key to implementing the composite construction.
- **Nayak (1994)** - Lexicographic revision operator, the least conservative admissible operator. Important as the other extreme of the admissibility spectrum from restrained revision.
- **Konieczny & Pino Perez (2000)** - Framework for iterated revision using distance semantics. Relevant to understanding how IC merging relates to the admissible revision operators defined here.
- **Friedman & Halpern (1999)** - Critique of belief revision that raises points about when the AGM framework is appropriate. Important for understanding the limitations of this approach.
