## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Li, Oren, Norman 2011 [10]** — Defines probabilistic argumentation frameworks with probability over structure (uncertain attacks/arguments). The structural complement to Thimm's epistemic approach. Already partially in collection.
- **Hunter 2012 [8]** — Foundations for probabilistic abstract argumentation. Companion paper presenting an alternative probabilistic formalization. Already in collection as Hunter 2017 (extended version).
- **Paris 1994 [11]** — The theoretical foundation for maximum entropy reasoning that Thimm's selection mechanism relies on.
- **Jaynes 2003 [9]** — Probability Theory: The Logic of Science. The broader philosophical grounding for entropy-based probability as an extension of logic.
- **Caminada 2006 [4]** — Reinstatement and labellings. The labelling framework that Thimm's postulates are derived from. Already in collection.
