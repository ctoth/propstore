Extends ASPIC+ with hypothetical reasoning and explanations, giving a structured-argumentation framework that can represent proof by contradiction, assumption attack, and argument-level explanation. The paper uses semantic paradoxes as its test domain and proves that the resulting framework preserves the expected rationality and non-triviality properties.
