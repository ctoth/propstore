## Why These Matter
- [[Dung_1995_AcceptabilityArguments]] is the abstract argumentation base the paper instantiates through ASPIC-END.
- [[Prakken_2010_AbstractFrameworkArgumentationStructured]] is the immediate ASPIC precursor that ASPIC-END extends with assumptions, explanation, and rule-attacks.
- [[Modgil_2014_ASPICFrameworkStructuredArgumentation]] is the tutorial-level ASPIC+ reference for the rationality postulates this paper reuses.
- [[Caminada_2006_IssueReinstatementArgumentation]] is a nearby formal semantics reference for the closure-and-labelling style of reasoning that ASPIC-END also relies on.
- [[Prakken_2006_FormalSystemsPersuasionDialogue]] is a useful broader structured-argumentation reference for how these frameworks support dialogical interaction, even though this paper's focus is different.
