- **Li et al. 2011** — Original PrAF definition with independence assumption; this is what propstore implements. Already in collection.
- **Riveret et al. 2018** — Labelling Framework for Probabilistic Argumentation combining epistemic + constellation approaches. Not in collection.
- **Fazzinga et al. 2018a** — Credulous and skeptical acceptability complexity results for probabilistic AFs. Directly relevant to propstore's MC sampling approach. Not in collection.
- **Timmer et al. 2015** — ASPIC+-based argumentation for Bayesian network explanation. Connects to propstore's ASPIC+ bridge. Not in collection.
- **Potyka 2019** — Polynomial-time fragment of epistemic probabilistic argumentation. Could inform efficient implementation. Not in collection.
