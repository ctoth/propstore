Comprehensive survey of probabilistic argumentation distinguishing epistemic (belief uncertainty over fixed topology) from constellation (topology uncertainty via subgraph distributions) approaches, with formal definitions of credulous/skeptical acceptance probability, extension probability, and rationality postulates (COH, RAT, FOU, TRU, OPT). Provides the authoritative taxonomy for propstore's PrAF implementation and identifies limitations of the independence assumption used in Li et al. 2011's PrAF model.
