## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Halpern and Pearl 2001/2005: baseline original and updated definitions this paper modifies.
- Hopkins and Pearl 2003: source of the counterexample that motivated the updated HP definition and remains central here.
- Aleksandrowicz et al. 2014 and Eiter and Lukasiewicz 2002: complexity baselines for original and updated HP causality.
- Halpern and Hitchcock 2015: normality/defaults layer that Halpern treats as complementary to the modified definition.
- Hall 2007 and Hitchcock 2001: path-based alternatives used for comparison in Section 4.
