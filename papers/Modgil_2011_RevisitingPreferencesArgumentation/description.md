This IJCAI 2011 paper revises ASPIC+ so conflict-freeness is defined over attacks while preferences only determine defeat and acceptability. It also broadens the abstract framework to cover classical-logic instantiations and proves that the standard rationality postulates still hold under the revised setup. It is directly relevant to propstore because it gives the concise formal case for implementing preference handling at the defeat layer rather than erasing attacks inside the conflict-free definition.
