## Key Citations for Follow-up

**[17] Giunchiglia, Serafini & Simpson — Hierarchical meta-logics: intuitions, proof theory and semantics (1992).** The semantics companion to this paper — gives the "theories are partial descriptions of the unique world" semantics promised on p.47. **Already in collection at `papers/Giunchiglia_1992_HierarchicalMeta-logics/`.** Re-read with 1994 in hand to cross-check which properties transfer to the model-theoretic side.

**[33] McCarthy — Notes on formalizing context (IJCAI-93).** The `ist(c, p)` paper that Giunchiglia-Serafini operationalize. Direct link to propstore's context layer. **Already in the collection.** This 1994 paper is the computational companion to McCarthy's philosophical framing.

**[15] Giunchiglia & Serafini — Hierarchical meta-logics: some proof theoretical results (Trento Tech. Report 1993).** Contains the full proofs (this paper only hints). Needed if propstore wants to reuse the sublevel-property proof structure for its own soundness arguments about context-local reasoning.

**[11] Giunchiglia — Multilanguage systems (AAAI 1991).** The original ML systems paper; gives broader motivation and the framework setup that this 1994 paper refines and specializes to the MR-class.

**[13] Giunchiglia — Contextual reasoning (1993).** Direct companion piece from the same author connecting ML systems to context-based reasoning generally, including non-modal examples. Relevant if propstore wants the full contextual-reasoning framing rather than only the modal-logic embedding.

**[18] Giunchiglia & Smaill — Reflection in constructive and non-constructive automated reasoning (1989).** Origin of the reflection-up/reflection-down bridge-rule formulation. Implementation-level provenance for the bridge rule primitive.

**[37] Prawitz — Natural Deduction: A Proof Theoretical Study (1965).** The proof-theoretic vocabulary underlying the whole paper. Relevant if propstore ever implements a ND-style argument builder in its argumentation layer.
