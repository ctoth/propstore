## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Provan 1987** — "Efficiency Analysis of Multiple-Context TMSs in Scene Representation." Directly addresses performance/complexity of multi-context TMS, which is the core scalability concern for propstore's ATMS with branch assumptions.
- **Konolige 1986** — "A Deductive Model of Belief." Formal treatment of agent belief including the key observation that agents cannot derive all logically possible conclusions, making global consistency detection theoretically impossible.
- **deKleer 1986** — "An Assumption Based TMS." The foundational ATMS paper that DATMS extends. Already in our collection.
- **Smith and Davis 1981** — "Frameworks for Cooperation in Distributed Problem Solving." The result-sharing model that DATMS modifies. Relevant for understanding the baseline cooperative problem solving paradigm.
- **Stallman and Sussman 1971** — "Forward Reasoning and Dependency-Directed-Backtracking." Foundational work on dependency-directed backtracking that underlies both TMS and ATMS designs.
