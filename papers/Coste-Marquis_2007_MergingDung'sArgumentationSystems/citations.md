## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Konieczny & Pino Perez 2002 [19]**: IC merging framework for propositional belief bases — the direct foundation this paper lifts to the AF level. Essential for understanding the merging postulates.
- **Dung 1995 [13]**: The foundational paper on abstract argumentation frameworks. Already in collection.
- **Cayrol, Devred, Lagasquie-Schiex 2003 [28]**: Collective argumentation and disjunctive programming — predecessor work by some of the same authors on group argumentation.
- **Bondarenko et al. 1997 [4]**: Abstract argumentation-theoretic approach to default reasoning — connects Dung-style argumentation to assumption-based frameworks.
- **Prakken & Vreeswijk 2002 [26]**: Comprehensive handbook chapter on logics for defeasible argumentation — contextualizes this work within the broader argumentation landscape.
