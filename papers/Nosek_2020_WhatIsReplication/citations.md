## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Schmidt 2009 (Ref 1):** Comprehensive review arguing replication is neglected in social sciences — provides historical context for the replication crisis framing
- **Lakatos 1976 (Ref 12):** Falsification and methodology of scientific research programmes — philosophical foundation for viewing replication as theory confrontation rather than procedure repetition
- **Crandall & Sherman 2016 (Ref 9):** Argues for superiority of conceptual replications — directly contrasts with Nosek & Errington's position that the direct/conceptual distinction is misleading
- **Shadish et al. 2002 (Ref 11):** Experiments and quasi-experimental designs for generalized causal inference — foundational text on generalizability that informs the U/T/O/S framework
