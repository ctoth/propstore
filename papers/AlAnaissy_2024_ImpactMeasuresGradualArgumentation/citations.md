## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Delobelle and Villata 2019/2020 [15]:** Original impact measure paper that this work revises. Essential for understanding the baseline.
- **Kampik et al. 2024 [16]:** Contribution functions for QBAFs -- parallel line of work on explaining QBAF outcomes using different formalism.
- **Yun et al. 2023 [29]:** Argument attribution explanations in QBAFs -- directly related Shapley-based approach in bipolar setting.
- **Rago et al. 2016 [23]:** DF-QuAD semantics paper -- already in propstore collection, the semantics not studied in this impact measure paper.
- **Pu et al. 2021 [35]:** Counting semantics -- the only semantics satisfying both Independence and Directionality for both measures.
