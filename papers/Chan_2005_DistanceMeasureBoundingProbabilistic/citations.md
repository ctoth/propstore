## Key Citations for Follow-up

- [2] Chan & Darwiche 2001 "When do numbers really matter?" — Earlier UAI paper where the distance concept was first introduced; precursor to the IJAR version
- [4] Darwiche 2000 "A differential approach to inference" — Foundational sensitivity analysis framework for Bayesian networks using partial derivatives
- [9] Jeffrey 1965 "The Logic of Decision" — Original source for Jeffrey's rule of probability revision, shown here to minimize CD-distance
- [15] Pearl 1988 "Probabilistic Reasoning in Intelligent Systems" — Source of virtual evidence method, also shown to minimize CD-distance
- [13] Kullback & Leibler 1951 "On information and sufficiency" — KL-divergence definition, contrasted extensively with CD-distance in Section 3
