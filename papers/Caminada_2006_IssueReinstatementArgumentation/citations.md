## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Dung 1995**: Foundational paper defining argumentation frameworks and all standard semantics -- essential background for propstore's AF layer
- **[10] Caminada 2006 Technical Report**: Contains full proofs omitted from this paper -- needed if implementing and verifying the labelling theorems
- **[12] Verheij 2003**: Admissible stage extensions, the precursor concept to semi-stable semantics
- **[13] Jakobovits & Vermeir 1999**: Robust semantics -- an alternative approach to dealing with the same issues this paper addresses
- **[7] Pollock 1987**: Defeasible reasoning -- foundational for understanding rebutting vs undercutting defeat in structured argumentation
