Establishes a formal correspondence between three-valued argument labellings (in/out/undec) and Dung's standard argumentation semantics, showing that each semantics corresponds to a specific restriction on reinstatement labellings. Introduces semi-stable semantics as the labelling with minimal undecided arguments, filling a gap between preferred and stable semantics. Directly relevant to propstore's argumentation layer for choosing resolution strategies and implementing extension-to-labelling conversions.
