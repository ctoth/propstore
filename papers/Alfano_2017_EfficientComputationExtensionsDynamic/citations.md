## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[Alfano, Greco, Parisi 2019]** — Extended journal version in Group Decision and Negotiation journal with more detailed grounded extension algorithm. Would provide fuller algorithmic detail for implementation.
- **[Liao et al., 2011]** — Division-based approach to dynamic argumentation. Different strategy (structural decomposition) for the same problem. Already partially covered in collection.
- **[Greco and Parisi, 2016a]** — Earlier ECAI version focusing on deterministic extensions only. Predecessor to this work.
- **[Boella et al., 2009a]** — Dynamics with single extensions, additions only. Already in collection. Provides theoretical foundation this paper extends.
- **[Cayrol et al., 2014]** — Change in AFs by adding arguments. Already in collection. Different perspective (characterizing change vs. efficient recomputation).
