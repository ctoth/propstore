## Key Citations for Follow-up

- [19] Dung 1995. The paper's entire construction is a weighted extension of Dung's abstract argumentation framework and characteristic-function semantics.
- [10] Bench-Capon 2003. Core reference for value-based frameworks, one of the main comparison points for externally imposed priorities over attacks.
- [6] Baroni, Dunne, Giacomin 2009. Main reference for resolution-based grounded semantics, which Section 7.2 compares against weighted grounded semantics.
- [33] Matt and Toni 2008. Important alternate notion of argument strength based on game-theoretic interaction rather than externally assigned attack weights.
- [35] Modgil 2009 and [25] Dunne, Modgil, Bench-Capon 2010. Central EAF references for the meta-attack comparison in Section 7.3.
