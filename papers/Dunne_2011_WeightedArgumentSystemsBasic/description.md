Introduces weighted argument systems by attaching externally supplied positive weights to attacks and interpreting an inconsistency budget as the total attack weight that may be ignored. Develops weighted grounded, admissible, and preferred semantics together with hardness results, optimization formulations, and tractable subclasses for minimal-budget reasoning. Directly relevant to propstore because it is the core attack-weighting paper for budgeted acceptance under externally assigned weights rather than structurally derived strength.
