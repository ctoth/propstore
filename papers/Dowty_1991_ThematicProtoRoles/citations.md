## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Krifka 1987, 1989** - Provides the formal semantics of Incremental Theme via nominal reference and temporal constitution; directly formalizes Dowty's homomorphism claim
- **Foley & Van Valin 1984** - Actor/Undergoer macroroles are the closest competing framework to Proto-Agent/Proto-Patient; detailed comparison would clarify relative strengths
- **Croft 1986b** - Causal chain analysis of case syncretisms across 40 languages; provides the typological evidence that proto-role entailments cluster along the Agent/Patient divide
- **Clark & Carpenter 1989** - Language acquisition evidence for emergent semantic categories similar to Proto-Agent; relevant to cognitive grounding of proto-roles
- **Tenny 1987, 1988** - Aspectual interface hypothesis that aspectual delimitedness is the key semantic factor in argument selection; directly competing with Dowty's broader entailment-based account
