Čyras and Toni introduce ABA+, extending assumption-based argumentation with a transitive preference relation over assumptions. The key mechanism is attack reversal: attacks supported by assumptions strictly less preferred than the attacked assumption are blocked and reversed. This is the primary source for WS-O-arg-aba-adf's flat ABA+ preference-filtered attack semantics.
