## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dempster, A. P. (1967-1968)** — "Upper and lower probabilities induced by a multivalued mapping" and related work. The direct predecessor to this book; Shafer reinterprets and extends Dempster's framework.
- **Choquet, G.** — Theory of capacities. The mathematical foundation for belief functions as non-additive set functions (alternating capacities of infinite order).
- **Iverson et al. (1971)** — Psychometrika article analyzing dice data. Concrete example of applying chance theory.
- **Denoeux, T. (2018)** — "Decision-Making with Belief Functions: A Review" — Modern survey of DS theory applications (already in propstore papers collection).
- **Josang, A. (2001)** — "A Logic for Uncertain Probabilities" — Subjective logic building on DS theory (already in propstore papers collection).
