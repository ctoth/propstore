This paper presents lemon (Lexicon Model for Ontologies), a modular RDF model for linking lexical entries to ontology entities, separating "word knowledge" from "world knowledge." The core model provides lexical entry, form, and sense primitives, with opt-in modules for morphology, phrase structure, syntax-semantics mapping, and variation. The model's strict separation of linguistic and ontological layers parallels propstore's separation of claims from concepts, though propstore's argumentation layer goes significantly beyond lexicalization.
