## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **McCrae et al. 2011 (Linking Lexical Resources...)** — The full conference paper for lemon; contains the detailed formal specification that this workshop abstract summarizes
- **Guarino 1998 (Formal Ontology in Information Systems)** — Foundational reference for ontology theory; defines the conceptual framework lemon builds upon
- **Buitelaar 2010 (Ontology-based Semantic Lexicon)** — Prior work by the lead author on mapping terms to object descriptions; direct predecessor to lemon
- **McCrae et al. (forthcoming, Interchanging lexical resources)** — Comprehensive treatment of lemon for interchange; likely contains evaluation and broader comparisons
