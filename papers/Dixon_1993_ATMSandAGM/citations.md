## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[Gardenfors, 1988]** -- The definitive AGM reference; essential for understanding the belief revision axioms and operators used throughout this paper.
- **[Gardenfors and Makinson, 1988]** -- Defines the epistemic entrenchment relation (EE1-EE5) that is central to the translation algorithm; the axioms are directly implemented.
- **[de Kleer, 1986]** -- The original ATMS paper; already in our collection. The functional specification from this paper is what Dixon & Foo prove equivalence against.
- **[Williams, 1993]** -- Finite theory base approach to AGM; Dixon implemented this (see [Dixon, 1993]), solving the infinite closed theory problem.
- **[Reinfrank et al., 1989]** -- Another bridge paper connecting TMS to a logic (autoepistemic), useful for understanding the broader landscape of TMS-logic connections.
