## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[2] Adams 1975 — *The Logic of Conditionals*.** Probabilistic semantics that system P strictly extends. Foundational for understanding why probabilistic readings invalidate Cut and Cautious Monotonicity (KLM p.12).
- **[20] Lehmann 1989 — "What does a conditional knowledge base entail?"** Solves the gap KLM leave open (e.g. inferring a∧p|~e when a is unmentioned in K). Introduces rational closure / ranked models. Direct sequel.
- **[22] Lehmann-Magidor 1988 TR / 1992 paper — Rational logics.** Representation result for Rational Monotonicity over ranked models. KLM defer this proof here.
- **[28] Makinson 1988 — "General theory of cumulative inference."** Independent and parallel development of cumulative reasoning. Coined "cumulative."
- **[34] Pearl-Geffner 1988 — Probabilistic semantics for default reasoning.** Built on Adams + system P; bridges probabilistic and preferential views. Already in propstore via Pearl 2009 if collected.
- **[39] Shoham 1988 — *Reasoning about Change*.** Original (flawed) preferential-model semantics; KLM fix the smoothness/labeling issue. Worth reading to see what KLM corrected.
- **[10] Gabbay 1985 — "Theoretical foundations..."** Origin of Reflexivity + Cut + CM as the rock bottom of nonmonotonic inference; KLM's system C closely matches.
