## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Halpin et al. 2010** -- "When owl:sameAs isn't the same" -- foundational analysis of sameAs semantics in Linked Data, directly complementary to de Melo's computational approach
- **Ding et al. 2010** -- "owl:sameAs and Linked Data: An empirical study" -- large-scale empirical analysis of sameAs usage patterns
- **Garg, Vazirani, and Yannakakis 1996** -- LP relaxation technique for multicut problems -- the algorithmic foundation for de Melo's approach
- **Euzenat and Shvaiko 2007** -- *Ontology matching* textbook -- broader context for entity alignment and identity resolution
- **Ding et al. 2010b** -- SameAs networks and beyond -- analyzing degree/nature and implications of owl:sameAs at scale
