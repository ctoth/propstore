## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Baumann 2012 [11,12]** — Foundational work on extension enforcement via minimal structural change. Essential for understanding the basic enforcement operators.
- **Coste-Marquis et al. 2014-2015 [31,33,34]** — Extension enforcement as optimization, belief revision for AFs. Provides the computational framework and complexity results.
- **Dimopoulos, Mailly, Moraitis 2018 [38]** — Control Argumentation Frameworks. Handles uncertainty in enforcement, directly relevant to propstore's incomplete-information scenario.
- **Wallner, Niskanen, Jarvisalo 2017 [66]** — Complexity results and algorithms for enforcement. Important for understanding computational feasibility.
- **Diller et al. 2018 [37]** — Families of revision operators for AFs. Provides parameterized framework connecting belief revision theory to argumentation.
