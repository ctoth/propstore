## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dung 1995 [2]**: Foundation for all abstract argumentation — already in propstore's literature base
- **Agresti & Coull 1998 [17]**: The confidence interval method used for MC convergence — essential for implementing the adaptive stopping criterion
- **Dunne et al. 2011 [21]**: Weighted argument systems — the main alternative approach (weights as strength rather than existence probability); important to understand the distinction
- **Hunter 2012 [14]**: Another probabilistic argumentation approach presented at the same venue — may offer complementary insights
- **Baroni et al. 2010 [23]**: Extension counting complexity — relevant for understanding when exact computation is feasible
