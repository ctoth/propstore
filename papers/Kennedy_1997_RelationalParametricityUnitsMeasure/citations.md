## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Kennedy 1995, *Programming Languages and Dimensions* - fuller thesis treatment of dimensions, likely the best next source for scaling-invariance details.
- Kennedy 1994, "Dimension types" - earlier implementation-oriented account of dimension types.
- Wand and O'Keefe 1991, "Automatic dimensional inference" - prior inference system for unit/dimension annotations.
- Rittri 1995, "Dimension inference under polymorphic recursion" - source for the relative definability trick Kennedy uses for geometric mean.
- Birkhoff 1960, *Hydrodynamics* - source Kennedy cites for the classical Pi theorem.
