## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[6] de Moura & Bjørner (2008) - Z3: An Efficient SMT Solver** - The base system that νZ extends; essential background for understanding the architecture.
- **[4] Cimatti et al. (2013) - Modular approach to MaxSAT modulo theories** - OptiMathSAT, a competing/complementary approach to optimization in SMT.
- **[12] Narodytska & Bacchus (2014) - Core-Guided MaxSAT resolution** - The MaxRes algorithm that νZ implements; key algorithmic contribution.
- **[14] Rayside et al. (2009) - Guided Improvement Algorithm** - The Pareto front algorithm adapted in νZ's Algorithm 4.
- **[15] Sebastiani & Tomasi (2012) - Optimization in SMT with LA(Q)** - Alternative approach to linear arithmetic optimization in SMT.
