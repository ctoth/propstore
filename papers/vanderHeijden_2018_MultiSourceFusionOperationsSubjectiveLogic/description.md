Provides corrected multi-source fusion operators for subjective logic, fixing base rate errors in Josang 2016 for cumulative and averaging belief fusion, and establishing formal N-source definitions for weighted belief fusion (WBF), consensus & compromise fusion (CCF), and belief constraint fusion (BCF). The paper proves WBF equivalence to confidence-weighted averaging of Dirichlet hyper-probability distributions, providing a verification backbone for opinion algebra implementations. Directly relevant to propstore's subjective logic layer, particularly for verifying correctness of multi-source fusion and base rate handling.
