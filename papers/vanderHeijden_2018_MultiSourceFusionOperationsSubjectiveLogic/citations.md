## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Jøsang 2016 [8]**: The reference book on subjective logic — contains the formulas this paper corrects. Essential context for understanding base rate errors in CBF/ABF multi-source fusion.
- **Jøsang 2001 [7]**: Original subjective logic paper defining opinion algebra — already in propstore collection. Foundation for everything in this paper.
- **Jøsang 2014 [24]**: "Cumulative and averaging fusion of beliefs" — the journal paper that introduced CBF and ABF, which this paper extends and corrects for multi-source.
- **Jøsang & McAnally 2004 [11]**: Multiplication and comultiplication of beliefs — referenced in propstore CLAUDE.md as not yet implemented.
- **Shafer 1976 [22]**: Mathematical Theory of Evidence — Dempster-Shafer foundations relevant to BCF. Already in propstore collection.
