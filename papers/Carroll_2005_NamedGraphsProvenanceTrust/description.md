Extends RDF with Named Graphs — pairs (URIref, RDF graph) — and defines a Semantic Web Publishing (SWP) vocabulary for authorities, warrants, digital signatures, and the distinction between asserting vs quoting a graph.
Contributes abstract syntax, three concrete syntaxes (TriX, TriG, RDF/XML), formal model-theoretic semantics extending RDF(S)/OWL with performative warrants, and an illustrative (sound but incomplete) trust-policy algorithm for information consumers.
Grounds propstore's requirement that provenance ride alongside knowledge statements without collapsing disagreement at storage time; supplies the formal vocabulary for the asserted-vs-quoted distinction that separates agent commitments from heuristic proposals.
