## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Castillo et al. 1997 [2]** — Symbolic sensitivity analysis for discrete BNs; alternative approach using algebraic propagation rather than the functional-form characterization
- **Laskey 1995 [12]** — Earlier work on BN sensitivity analysis that the authors build upon and critique; established that some CPTs are uninfluential
- **van der Gaag & Renooij 2001 [19]** — Follow-up work by the same group on analyzing the sensitivity data once computed; practical methods for interpreting results
- **Pradhan et al. 1996 [16]** — Experimental investigation of BN sensitivity to imprecise probabilities; provides empirical evidence for the robustness findings
- **Pearl 1988 [15]** — Foundational BN reference; defines the probabilistic inference framework used throughout
