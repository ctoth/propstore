## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Doyle [5]**: The original TMS paper that McDermott's work directly extends and synthesizes with data pools. Essential companion reading.
- **de Kleer et al. [4]**: Early work on explicit control of reasoning and dependency-directed backtracking at MIT, which influenced both Doyle and McDermott.
- **McAllester [11]**: Proposes an alternative approach to truth maintenance; McAllester pointed out that labels can grow exponentially with depth of well-founded support.
- **Stallman and Sussman [16, 19]**: First to identify the correspondence between data pool beads and dependency premisses; pioneered forward reasoning and dependency-directed backtracking.
- **Charniak, Riesbeck, and McDermott [2]**: Textbook containing implementation details for data dependency systems discussed in the paper.
