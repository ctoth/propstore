## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Alchourrón, Gärdenfors, and Makinson (1985)** — The foundational AGM paper on partial meet contraction. Already in collection as `Alchourron_1985_TheoryChange`.
- **Gärdenfors (1988) *Knowledge in Flux*** — Book-length treatment with more detailed proofs and additional results on epistemic entrenchment. Would provide fuller context for implementation.
- **Makinson (1987)** — Discusses recovery postulate (K-5), which is the most controversial AGM postulate. Important for understanding when the framework's assumptions may not hold.
- **Martins and Shapiro (1986)** — Alternative belief revision foundations from TARK 1, connects to SNePS/reason maintenance systems which relate to ATMS-style approaches.
- **Foo and Rao (1986) "DYNABELS"** — Label-based approach to belief revision, potentially connecting to ATMS-style justification tracking.
