## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dung 1995** - Supplies the abstract acceptability framework that this paper treats as too coarse for internal argument revision, making it the baseline contrast for the proposed dynamics.
- **Falappa, Simari, and Chesñevar 2002** - The closest prior bridge between belief revision and defeasible reasoning; this paper extends that line from explanatory support to direct warrant-oriented argument change.
- **Levi 1977** - Provides the revision identity that motivates the paper's argument-change identity and the sequence expansion-then-contraction.
- **Alchourrón, Gärdenfors, and Makinson 1985** - The AGM foundation behind the contraction/revision decomposition and minimal-change viewpoint.
- **Martinez, Garcia, and Simari 2007** - Provides the well-structured argumentation-line machinery reused in the paper's definition of attacking lines and dialectical manipulation.
