Defines an abstract theory of argument change that adapts AGM-style revision ideas to structured arguments with active/inactive status, subargument structure, and dialectical constraints.
Its key technical contribution is a warrant-prioritized revision operator built from expansion plus incision-based contraction under cautiousness and preservation conditions.
This is highly relevant to propstore because it offers a principled recipe for minimally modifying argumentative support so a target argument becomes warranted.
