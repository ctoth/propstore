## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dung 1995 [11]**: The abstract acceptability semantics this paper extends directly; needed to compare where the preference layer changes the fixpoint and defence conditions.
- **Amgoud and Cayrol 1998 [3]**: Immediate predecessor on preference-based acceptability; useful for seeing what this 2002 paper generalizes or revises.
- **Prakken and Sartor 1996/1997 [19, 20]**: The main contrast class for prioritized argumentation and dialogue proof theory; the paper repeatedly positions its own framework against these approaches.
- **Simari and Loui 1992 [21]**: Foundational defeasible reasoning treatment cited in the abstract framework and defeat discussions.
- **Amgoud, Parsons, and Perrussel 2000 [8]**: The contextual-preferences extension explicitly named in the conclusion as the path beyond the total-preorder assumption.
