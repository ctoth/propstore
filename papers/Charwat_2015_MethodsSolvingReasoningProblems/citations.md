## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Dung 1995**: Foundational paper defining abstract argumentation frameworks and all standard semantics. Essential background for any implementation work.
- **[7] Modgil & Prakken 2014 (ASPIC+)**: The structured argumentation framework that builds on Dung AFs. Critical for understanding how abstract solvers connect to practical argumentation.
- **[25] Dvorak et al. 2014**: Complexity-sensitive decision procedures -- shows how to choose algorithms based on the complexity class of the specific problem instance.
- **[47] Egly, Gaggl, Woltran 2010**: The ASPARTIX system's ASP encodings for argumentation -- reference implementation for ASP-based argumentation reasoning.
- **[26] Cerutti et al. 2014**: Computing preferred extensions with SCC-based decomposition -- algorithmic optimization relevant to practical implementations.
