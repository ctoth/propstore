## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Modgil & Prakken 2018** — The definitive ASPIC+ framework chapter; needed to understand the full formalism with preferences that this paper restricts away
- **Lehtonen, Wallner & Järvisalo 2019** — Their prior work on ASP for ABA; shows the template this ASPIC+ work builds on
- **Dung 1995** — Foundation for all abstract argumentation; the AF semantics that Theorem 5 connects to assumptions
- **Lehtonen, Odekerken, Wallner & Järvisalo 2024** — Follow-up handling preferences (last-link principle); extends this work to the full ASPIC+ with preference orderings
- **Odekerken, Lehtonen, Borg, Wallner & Järvisalo 2023** — Follow-up on incomplete information in ASPIC+; addresses stability and relevance problems
