## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Pustejovsky (1991, book) - *The Generative Lexicon: A Theory of Computational Lexical Semantics*:** The full book-length treatment of this theory, with detailed formalizations omitted from the journal article.
- **Copestake and Briscoe (1991) - "Lexical operations in a unification-based framework":** Addresses formal derivability of inheritance information during composition, which this paper defers.
- **Grimshaw (1990) - *Argument Structure*:** Provides the hierarchically structured argument structure theory that this paper builds upon for its syntax-semantics mapping.
- **Moravcsik (1975) - "Aitia as generative factor in Aristotle's philosophy":** The philosophical source for the four qualia roles central to this theory.
- **Hobbs et al. (1987) - "Commonsense metaphysics and lexical semantics":** Integration of lexical semantics with commonsense knowledge representation, relevant to propstore's knowledge integration goals.
