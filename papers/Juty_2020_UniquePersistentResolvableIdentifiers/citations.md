- **Wilkinson et al. 2016 [1]** — The original FAIR Guiding Principles paper; foundational reference for understanding what FAIR requires

- **Wimalaratne et al. 2018 [23]** — Uniform resolution of compact identifiers for biomedical data; the technical implementation paper for identifiers.org compact identifiers
- **Fenner et al. 2019 [16]** — Data citation roadmap for scholarly data repositories; practical implementation guidance for data citation
- **Kunze & Rodgers 2008 [21]** — The ARK Identifier Scheme; technical specification for the ARK system
