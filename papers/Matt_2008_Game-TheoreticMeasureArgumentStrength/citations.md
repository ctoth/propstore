## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Cayrol & Lagasquie-Schiex 2005** (ref 8): The main competing gradual semantics approach based on "interaction" and "categoriser" functions — essential comparison point for understanding alternative strength measures.
- **Dung 1995** (ref 13): Foundational paper on abstract argumentation frameworks — already in the project's literature base.
- **Besnard & Hunter 2001** (ref 7): Logic-based deductive arguments with strength — another approach to graded argumentation that uses structured rather than abstract arguments.
- **Rahwan & Larson 2008** (ref 25): Mechanism design for abstract argumentation — another game-theoretic approach but focused on incentive compatibility rather than strength measurement.
- **Baroni & Giacomin 2005/2008** (refs 5, 27): SCC-recursiveness and evaluation of argumentation semantics — provides the principle-based evaluation framework against which gradual measures should be tested.
