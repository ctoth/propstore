## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Prinz et al. 2011** (Ref 5): Bayer HealthCare's independent study reporting ~25% reproducibility of published preclinical findings — the key corroborating evidence for Begley & Ellis's claims.
- **Hutchinson & Kirk 2011** (Ref 2): Analysis of high drug attrition rates in oncology — provides context for why irreproducible preclinical research has downstream clinical consequences.
- **Hait 2010** (Ref 1): Overview of the "grand challenges" in anticancer drug development — frames the broader problem space.
- **Tentler et al. 2012** (Ref 12): Review of patient-derived xenograft models as potentially more predictive preclinical tools — directly relevant to one of Begley & Ellis's proposed solutions.
