Analyzes how ASPIC+-style structured argumentation lines up with classical nonmonotonic-reasoning axioms by studying two consequence relations: one for argument construction and one for justified conclusions. The main result is that ASPIC+ is cumulative but not monotonic at the argument-construction level, and even weaker at the justified-conclusion level unless the theory is purely strict. Directly relevant for propstore because it turns ASPIC+ behavior into a concrete property checklist for entailment and proof-status implementations.
