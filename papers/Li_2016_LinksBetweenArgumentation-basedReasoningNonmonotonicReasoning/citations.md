## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Kraus, Lehmann, and Magidor 1990** - Source of the Ref/LLE/RW/Cut/CM/M/T/CP axiom family used throughout the paper.
- **Modgil and Prakken 2014** - Canonical ASPIC+ tutorial defining the structured-argumentation machinery analyzed here.
- **Dung 1995** - Provides the abstract argumentation semantics used once the structured theory is turned into an AF.
- **Caminada, Modgil, and Oren 2014** - Supplies the unrestricted-rebut variant that this paper explicitly chooses not to use.
- **Li and Parsons 2015** - Immediate precursor on purely defeasible rules; this paper generalizes the analysis to mixed strict/defeasible theories.
