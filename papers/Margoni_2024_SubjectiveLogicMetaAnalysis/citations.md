## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Josang, A. (2016). Subjective Logic: A Formalism for Reasoning Under Uncertainty. Springer.** — The comprehensive reference for all SL operators including WBF; already partially implemented in propstore
- **Walkinshaw, N., & Hierons, R. (2023). Modelling second-order uncertainty in state machines. IEEE TSE.** — Extends SL to state machine modeling; may inform propstore's ATMS integration
- **Walkinshaw, N., & Shepperd, M. (2020). Reasoning about uncertainty in empirical results. EASE.** — Predecessor work that likely contains the initial framework for this approach
- **Camerer, C. F., et al. (2018). Evaluating the replicability of social science experiments. Nature Human Behaviour.** — Already in propstore collection; source of replication base rates used in this paper
- **Josang, A., & Hankin, R. (2012). Interpretation and fusion of hyper opinions in subjective logic.** — Hyper opinions extend beyond binomial; may be needed for multinomial propstore use cases
