## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Guha 1991** — The formal theory of contexts underlying CYC's microtheory system. Essential for understanding the theoretical foundations and the `ist` operator semantics.
- **McCarthy 1987/1990** — Original "Generality in AI" paper introducing the consistency problem and context-based solutions. Foundation for all context-based knowledge representation.
- **Buvac et al. 1995** — "Metamathematics of Contexts" — formal properties of context systems including the contextual omniscience property referenced in the paper.
- **Ramachandran et al. 2005** — CYC efficiency improvements for first-ordered data types, relevant to practical implementation of context-scoped reasoning.
- **Bohnea et al. 2004** — Prior PAKM paper on representing knowledge gaps in CYC, likely addresses the complementary problem of missing information (vs. contradictory information).
