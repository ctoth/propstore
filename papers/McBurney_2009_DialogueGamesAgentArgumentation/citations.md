## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Walton & Krabbe 1995** [96]: Foundational work on dialogue types and commitment in dialogue. Essential for understanding the typology used throughout.
- **Prakken 2006** [80]: Comprehensive review of formal persuasion dialogue systems. Important for understanding the state of the art in argumentation protocols.
- **McBurney & Parsons 2002** [66]: The formal PDL-based framework for agent dialogues. The technical companion to this survey chapter.
- **Hamblin 1970** [41]: Origin of commitment stores concept used throughout dialogue game literature.
- **McBurney, Parsons & Wooldridge 2002** [70]: Desiderata for agent argumentation protocols. Defines the design criteria discussed in Section 5.
