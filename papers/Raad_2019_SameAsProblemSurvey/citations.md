## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Halpin et al. 2010** — "When owl:sameAs Isn't the Same" — Foundational critique of sameAs semantics; introduced contextual identity concept. Essential for understanding the theoretical basis of the identity problem.
- **Beek et al. 2018** — "sameAs.cc: The Closure of 500M owl:sameAs Statements" — Large-scale empirical analysis of the entire owl:sameAs network. Provides concrete data on identity closure sizes and error propagation.
- **de Melo 2013** — "Not Quite the Same: Identity Constraints for the Web of Linked Data" — Proposes alternative identity constraints; relevant to propstore's graded concept identity.
- **Raad et al. 2018** — "Constructing and Evaluating Identity Links on the Web Using Network Statistics" — Network-based approach to identity link quality; could inspire propstore's concept merge quality metrics.
- **Valdestilhas et al. 2017** — "Cedal: Time-efficient Detection of Erroneous Links" — Combined terminological + structural detection; practical approach adaptable to concept identity verification.
