Surveys the owl:sameAs identity problem in the Web of Data, covering how identity links are created, why many are erroneous due to semantic overloading and transitivity, and what approaches exist for detecting and repairing them. Categorizes detection approaches (terminological, semantic, network-based, content-based) and alternative identity predicates that relax formal sameAs semantics. Directly relevant to propstore's concept-merging challenge: identity should be contextual and policy-driven rather than globally collapsed, aligning with the non-commitment discipline at the semantic core.
