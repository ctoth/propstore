## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[6] de Kleer 1986 (ATMS)**: Already in collection. The main alternative approach to assumption-based reasoning that this paper compares against.
- **[12] Doyle 1979 (TMS)**: Already in collection. The foundational TMS paper that started the field; SWM/MBR is designed to improve upon Doyle's justification-based approach.
- **[25] Martins 1983 (Ph.D. Dissertation)**: The full dissertation containing detailed proofs and extended discussion of everything in this paper. Essential for understanding implementation details.
- **[49] Shapiro & Rapaport 1987 (SNePS)**: Describes SNePS as a fully intensional propositional semantic network, which is the representation substrate for SNeBR.
- **[15] Fitch 1952 (Symbolic Logic)**: The source of the natural deduction system (FR) that SWM's relevance logic extends, using subproofs and hypothesis/discharge structure.
