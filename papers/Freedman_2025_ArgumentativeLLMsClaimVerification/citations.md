## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Baroni, Rago, and Toni 2019** — "How many properties do we need for gradual argumentation?" Foundational theory for the properties (monotonicity, etc.) that this paper proves ArgLLMs satisfy. Essential for understanding what formal guarantees a QBAF semantics should provide.

- **Rago et al. 2016** — Original DF-QuAD paper. Need to read for the complete specification of the aggregation function and its proofs.

- **Cayrol and Lagasquie-Schiex 2005** — Bipolar argumentation frameworks. Theoretical foundation for QBAFs (support + attack). Already in propstore's literature grounding.

- **Amgoud, Ben-Naim, and Vesic 2017** — Acceptability semantics for weighted argumentation frameworks. Alternative semantics to DF-QuAD that may be worth comparing.

- **Noor, Rago, and Toni 2024** — ArgLLMs applied to medical decision-making. Shows domain-specific adaptation of the same pipeline.
