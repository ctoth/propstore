## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Euzenat, "An API for ontology alignment" and Alignment API 4.0: needed to understand the post-processing format and confidence surface used by BLOOMS.
- Euzenat and Shvaiko, "Ontology matching": foundation text for ontology matching terminology and evaluation.
- RiMOM, AROMA, S-Match, OMViaUO, CSR, and TaxoMap papers: comparator systems that clarify what BLOOMS improves over.
- Bizer et al. on DBpedia and Linked Data: context for why Wikipedia/DBpedia is a useful but noisy bootstrapping source.
- UMBEL documentation: related approach for schema-level reference frameworks in LOD.
