## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Pearl [1] (1988)** — *Probabilistic Reasoning in Intelligent Systems*. The book Wilson is responding to throughout; defines noisy-OR, sandwich principle, conditional-probability rule reading.
- **Shafer [7] (1987)** — *Probability judgment in AI and expert systems*. Source of the compatibility-relation extension reading of belief functions.
- **Dempster [16] (1967)** — Original upper/lower probabilities induced by multivalued mapping. Foundational for the [Bel, Pl] envelope reading.
- **Fagin & Halpern [15] (1990)** — "FH conditioning" Bel(A‖B) = inf P(A|B). Direct comparator to Dempster conditioning.
- **Wilson [21,22]** — Wilson's own prior work on Monte Carlo D-S and the rules-as-default-logic semantics that this paper builds on.
- **Geffner [24] (1989)** — Default reasoning thesis defining "irrelevance" predicate; Wilson cites for limit-case analysis of conditional-prob rules.
