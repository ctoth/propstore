## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[17] Kraus, Lehmann, Magidor 1990** — direct prequel: System P, preferential models, cumulative logics. Already collected; this paper assumes its results.
- **[27] Pearl 1990 (System Z)** — alternative tractable rational-closure-style construction in the same TARK proceedings; comparison with rational closure is acknowledged in Section 5.1 (p.27).
- **[11] Freund, Lehmann, Morris 1991** — follow-up on rationality, transitivity and contraposition; refines rationality landscape.
- **[1] Adams 1975** — primary probabilistic-conditional reference; Section 4.3 builds on this and provides an alternative completeness proof.
- **[20] Lehmann, Magidor 1990 (TARK)** — "Preferential logics: the predicate calculus case" — first steps for first-order extension, flagged in conclusion (p.42).
