## Why These Matter
- [[Dung_1995_AcceptabilityArguments]] is the abstract-semantics base for the paper's defeat graph.
- [[Prakken_2010_AbstractFrameworkArgumentationStructured]] supplies the ASPIC precursor formalism that this paper instantiates for case-based reasoning.
- [[Modgil_2014_ASPICFrameworkStructuredArgumentation]] is the later tutorial-level ASPIC+ reference that situates the metatheory used here.
- Prakken (2006) on persuasion dialogue and Wyner, Bench-Capon, and Atkinson (2011) on formalising argumentation about legal cases are part of the legal-argumentation scheme lineage this paper formalizes.
- Gordon and Walton (2009) and Wyner and Bench-Capon (2007) are the immediate scheme-level predecessors.
