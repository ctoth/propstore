## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dung, Kowalski, Toni 2006 [8]:** Direct antecedent of this paper's procedures; the new AB/IB/Fail derivations are explicit adaptations of the earlier admissibility procedure.
- **Dung 1995 [7]:** Supplies the abstract-argumentation definitions and semantics that ideal dispute trees compute.
- **Vreeswijk 2000 [21]:** Earlier computational treatment of preferred, grounded, and ideal semantics that this paper compares against in Section 5.
- **Kakas and Toni 1999 [14]:** Grounded-semantics proof procedure in logic programming used as a comparison point and partial precursor.
- **Bondarenko, Toni, Kowalski 1993 [4]:** Foundational ABA framework the paper instantiates and extends with sceptical ideal procedures.
