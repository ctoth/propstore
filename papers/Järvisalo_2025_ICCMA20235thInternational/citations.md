## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[16] Dung 1995** - Foundational paper on abstract argumentation frameworks. Already in the propstore collection as Dung_1995_AcceptabilityArguments. Essential reference for AF semantics definitions.
- **[10] Bondarenko et al. 1997** - Foundational paper on assumption-based argumentation (ABA). Important for understanding the ABA track formalism and how it relates to abstract AFs.
- **[137] Thimm & Villata 2017** - Previous ICCMA competition overview (ICCMA 2017), provides historical context and comparison with ICCMA 2023 developments.
- **[119] Niskanen & Järvisalo 2020** - mu-toksia solver description, one of the leading SAT-based approaches for argumentation solving that competed in ICCMA 2023.
- **[55] Dvořák et al. 2024** - ASPARTIX-DL system description, representing the ASP-based approach to argumentation solving.
