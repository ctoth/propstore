## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Baroni and Giacomin 2007** - Foundational source for the principle-based evaluation framework that this paper extends to dynamic abstraction.
- **Caminada 2006** - Supplies the accepted/rejected/undecided partition that makes the attack- and argument-abstraction principles precise.
- **Dung 1995** - The formal base framework on which every definition in this paper depends.
- **Cayrol et al. 2008** - Closest prior work on revision dynamics, useful for comparing general abstraction principles against operator-specific revision approaches.
- **Rotstein et al. 2008** - Gives the AGM-style dynamics perspective that this paper explicitly contrasts with its more general principle-based approach.
