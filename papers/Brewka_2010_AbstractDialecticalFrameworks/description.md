Introduces Abstract Dialectical Frameworks (ADFs), a generalization of Dung AFs where each node has an arbitrary propositional acceptance condition over its parents, enabling unified modeling of attack, support, and complex dependencies. Shows that grounded and complete semantics generalize to all ADFs while stable and preferred semantics require restriction to bipolar ADFs (BADFs), and demonstrates practical expressiveness through weighted ADFs that can model legal proof standards like preponderance of evidence and beyond reasonable doubt. Directly relevant to propstore's argumentation layer for modeling non-attack relationships and threshold-based acceptance.
