## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dung 1995 [10]:** The baseline semantics that prudent semantics refine.
- **Baroni and Giacomin 2003 [2]:** Directly relevant to the paper's treatment of odd-length cycles and indirect conflicts.
- **Cayrol, Doutre, and Mengin 2003 [5]:** Complexity background for preferred semantics and decision problems.
- **Doutre and Mengin 2004 [9]:** Sceptical vs. credulous acceptance background used in the complexity comparison.
- **Coste-Marquis, Devred, and Marquis 2005 [6]:** Companion paper on symmetric argumentation frameworks already present in this collection.
