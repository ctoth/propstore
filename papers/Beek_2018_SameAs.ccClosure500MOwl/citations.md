## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Halpin et al. (2010) [8]** — "When owl:sameAs isn't the same" — foundational analysis of identity confusion in Linked Data; directly relevant to understanding why transitive closure of owl:sameAs produces garbage identity sets
- **Raad et al. (2018) [14]** — Detecting erroneous identity links — the quality-filtering complement to this paper's closure computation; relevant to propstore's need to hold competing identity claims
- **Beek et al. (2014) [3]** — LOD Laundromat — the data infrastructure underlying this paper; explains how the LOD Cloud is crawled and normalized
- **Ding et al. (2010) [6]** — Earlier owl:sameAs deployment study at smaller scale; useful for understanding how identity linking practices evolved
- **Fernandez et al. (2017) [7]** — LOD-a-lot single-file HDT representation of the LOD Cloud; the actual data source queried in this paper
