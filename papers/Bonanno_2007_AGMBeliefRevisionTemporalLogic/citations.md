## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Alchourron, Gardenfors, Makinson 1985** - The original AGM theory paper. Already referenced aspirationally in propstore. Defines the postulates K*1-K*8 that this paper axiomatically characterizes.
- **[14] Kraus and Lehmann 1988** - Knowledge, belief and time. Alternative temporal approach using knowledge operator. Could inform how to handle the epistemic vs doxastic distinction in propstore's framework.
- **[10] Friedman and Halpern 1994** - Belief revision and iterated belief change using branching time without explicit information operator. Relevant for understanding how Bonanno 2012 builds on this to add plausibility orderings.
- **[17] Nayak, Pagnucco, Peppas 2003** - Dynamic belief revision operators. Relevant for understanding the operator-based approach to iterated revision.
- **[5] Bonanno 2005** - A simple modal logic for belief revision. The precursor to this paper; may contain additional semantic insights about QBR.
