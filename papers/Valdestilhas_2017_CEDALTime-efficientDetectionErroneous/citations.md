## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[10] Melo 2013:** Closest formal identity-constraint predecessor; useful for representing invalid `owl:sameAs` without immediately selecting a repair.
- **[14] Nentwig et al. 2014:** Describes LinkLion, the evaluated link repository and source of provenance/path information.
- **[21] Tarjan 1975:** Union-find foundation for the graph partitioning used by CEDAL.
- **[23] Valdestilhas et al.:** DBpedia identifier heterogeneity work by the first author; useful companion for same-dataset duplicate URI problems.
- **[26] Zaveri et al. 2015:** Data-quality dimensions used to frame semantic accuracy and consistency/conciseness errors.
