## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Smets 2005 [56]**: "Decision making in the TBM: the necessity of the pignistic transformation" — the definitive axiomatic treatment of why pignistic is the right point-estimate transformation. Essential for implementing the default render policy.

- **Gilboa and Schmeidler 1989 [31]**: "Maximin expected utility with non-unique prior" — axiomatic foundation for lower expected utility, directly applicable to conservative/pessimistic render policies.

- **Jaffray 1989 [38]**: "Linear utility theory for belief functions" — the axiomatic foundation for extending VNM utility theory to belief functions with the Hurwicz criterion. Needed if implementing parameterized risk attitudes.

- **Walley 1991 [69]**: "Statistical Reasoning with Imprecise Probabilities" — the comprehensive treatment of imprecise probability theory underlying maximality and E-admissibility criteria.

- **Shafer 2016 (referenced in text)**: "Constructive decision theory" — full treatment of the goals-based approach that constructs utility rather than assuming it, relevant for goal-directed queries.
